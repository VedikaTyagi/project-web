Innerve is back with Innerve Hacks which has been the flagship event of Innerve for the past 3 years. Innerve is proud to collaborate with Coding ninjas to organize one of the biggest pan-India hackathons to celebrate women in stem.

ELIGIBILITY

1. College/University enrolled students: UG/PG/PHD


2. Teams of 2 - 4 members should be made with 50% of the team including women.


Themes

This year, Innerve Hacks is collaborating with the Centre of Sustainable Development, IGDTUW. Innerve Hacks 2022 will be tackling 5 out of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals proposed by the United Nations and will be grouped under 3 broad themes as follows:

SDG-11
Sustainable city

SDG-3 & SDG-6
Good health, Clean Water and Sanitation

SDG-8 & SDG-9
Economic Growth, work, and Industrial development.

Mode of Competition

It will be a three staged competition with the final round being held offline in the IGDTUW campus.

Round 1

Preliminary Round


  • A technical test will be held online on the Coding Ninjas platform.
  • The syllabus includes Data Structures and Algorithms, Competitive Coding Problems, and objective questions on C++, Java, Python, HTML, CSS, etc.
  • All registered teams need to participate and the average of the team will be calculated

Round 2

Project Development Round


  • Shortlisted teams will be divided among 5 problem statements and be expected to develop a web/app/prototype of their solution
  • 2 evaluations will be done by Hackathon mentors for shortlisting teams on the basis of their progress.

Round 3

Finale Round


  • The top 50 teams will be shortlisted for the final round on 1st October 2022
  • Teams will have to report offline to the IGDTUW campus for the presentation round
